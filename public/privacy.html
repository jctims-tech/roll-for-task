Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Roll for Task does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Your Data Stays on Your Device

Your tasks, settings, and session information are stored locally on your device using your browser's built-in storage (localStorage). This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or anyone else. Clearing your browser data or uninstalling the app will remove it entirely.

Feedback Emails

If you choose to send feedback via the "Share feedback" link, your message is received and read by the app's creator and used only to improve the app. Your email address is never shared, sold, or added to any mailing list.

No Tracking

This app does not use cookies, analytics, advertising, or any third-party tracking of any kind.

No Accounts

Roll for Task does not require or offer user accounts. There is no login, no registration, and no personal information is ever requested.

Questions

If you have any questions about this policy, you can reach us at rollfortask@gmail.com.
